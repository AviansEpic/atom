Prefix: .
Be aware this script does not support display names use usernames instead when typing a players name

.give <gun> (Gives you the gun you specify)
Example: .give M9

.changeteam <team> (Changes your team to the specified team)
Example: .changeteam gaurd

.rejoin (Rejoins the server you are currently in)
Example: .rejoin

.serverhop (Joins a new server)
Example: .serverhop

.re (Resets your character)
Example: .re

.arrest <player (or all)> (Arrests the person you select or everyone)
Example: .arrest noobguy123

.to <player> (Teleports you to specified player)
Example: .to noobguy123

.noclip (Toggles noclip)
Example: .noclip

.walkspeed <number> (Sets your WalkSpeed/speed to the specified number)
Example: .walkspeed 30

.jumppower <number> (Sets your JumpPower to the specified number)
Example: .jumppower 100
